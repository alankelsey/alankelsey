The Cactus

Our featured cacti is the Christmas cactus. The Christmas cactus usually blooms around Christmas time.

Sometimes it blooms around New Years, but it is still called a Christmas cactus when that unforseen event occurs.

It grows in a pot in your house. Don't put it in the direct sun. And don't put it in a dark closet either.
