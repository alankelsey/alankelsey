Earth

DISTANCE FROM SUN: 93 million miles (159 million kilometers)
REVOLUTION AROUND SUN: 365.3 days
ROTATION: 23.9 days
DIAMETER: 7,926 miles (12,753 kilometers)
DENSITY: 5.5 x that of water
SURFACE TEMPERATURE: -128°F (-89°C) to 136°F (57.7°C).
Satalite:moon.

Even an alien flying into the solar system would have to imagine Earth as the immediate choice for the planet most likely to harbor life. Its blue expanse of ocean, its thin yet dynamic veil of atmosphere, its brown and green jigsaw of continents–all provide essential clues to the possibilities presented by planet Earth.
